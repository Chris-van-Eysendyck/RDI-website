The Approach

Hyperrealism over hype

In a world flooded with hot takes and punditry, Raven Data Insights cuts through the noise with a single discipline: placing geopolitical and financial predictions on the map — literally.

Every call is documented before the fact, dated, and publicly scored when it resolves. No cherry-picking. No revisionism. The raven sees all, and remembers.

My analysis draws on macroeconomics, geopolitical history, market structure, and the kind of cross-disciplinary thinking that siloed specialists rarely apply. I look for structural forces, not headlines — for the tectonic shifts that move slowly until, suddenly, they don't.

The goal is not to be right 100% of the time. The goal is to be rigorously honest about when I'm right, when I'm wrong, and why.
