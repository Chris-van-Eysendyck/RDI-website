The Method

Why an audit trail changes everything

Most geopolitical analysis is unfalsifiable by design. Consultancies sell narrative, not predictions. Hedged language. Scenario matrices with no commitments. When the dust settles, nobody checks who was right — because there's nothing to check.

We work differently. Every call is documented before the fact, dated, weighted by conviction, and scored publicly when it resolves. Hits and misses, side by side. The scorecard isn't a feature — it's the product.

The analysis itself draws on macroeconomics, geopolitical history, market structure, and the kind of cross-disciplinary thinking that siloed specialists rarely apply. We look for structural forces, not headlines — for the tectonic shifts that move slowly until, suddenly, they don't.

01

Predictions, not opinions

Every call is a falsifiable claim with a date and a region. If it can't be scored, it doesn't belong on the ledger.

02

Misses published

A track record that hides its failures is marketing. Ours includes every missed call, weighted and explained. That's how trust is built.

03

Structure over noise

We don't chase headlines. We map debt dynamics, demographic trajectories, energy dependencies, and alliance fragmentation — the forces that determine what happens next.

04

Built for decision-makers

Not academic papers. Not op-eds. Analysis designed for people who need to act — allocate capital, manage exposure, or brief a board — with confidence in the source.

The Approach

Hyperrealism over hype

In a world flooded with hot takes and punditry, Raven Data Insights cuts through the noise with a single discipline: placing geopolitical and financial predictions on the map — literally.

Every call is documented before the fact, dated, and publicly scored when it resolves. No cherry-picking. No revisionism. The raven sees all, and remembers.

The goal is not to be right 100% of the time. The goal is to be rigorously honest about when I'm right, when I'm wrong, and why.
