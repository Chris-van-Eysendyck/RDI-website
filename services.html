The Process

How it works

Three steps, no bureaucracy.

01

Brief us

Tell us what decision you're facing, what markets or regions concern you, and what timeframe matters. A 10-minute conversation or a short written brief is enough to scope the engagement.

02

We analyze

Every engagement is approached with the same discipline as our public track record: explicit probability-weighted scenarios, stated conviction levels, and conclusions that are documented before the fact — not retrofitted after.

03

Deliver & discuss

Written deliverable first. Then a call to walk through the reasoning, stress-test assumptions, challenge the conclusions, and answer questions. The analysis belongs to you.

On Pricing

A word on value

The market rate for analysis without accountability

The major geopolitical intelligence firms — Eurasia Group, Control Risks, Oxford Analytica — charge €25,000–€200,000 for custom research, and €5,000–€20,000 per month for retainer access. They do not publish their track records. Most don't keep one.

Our prices reflect a different proposition: rigorous, documented analysis from an analyst with a public, auditable track record — at a price that makes sense for organisations that need serious geopolitical intelligence but cannot justify a six-figure subscription. The analysis is the same quality. The accountability is higher. The cost is a fraction.

All prices above are indicative. Every engagement is scoped individually — contact us to discuss your specific needs.
